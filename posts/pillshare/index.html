Track your pills intake and vitals with Pillshare

As we all know we’re living in the time where diseases, doesn’t matter whether it’s acute or chronic, have no age. It can grip us anytime. So, the only thing we can do to get away from it is to monitor our vitals frequently.

Why do you like Pillshare ?

There are many products that are available in the market now. Some are not affordable for the majority and therefore they go for the cheaper ones. In reality, the cheaper ones might not give accurate results which can even end up in death. This is the serious phase of every medical product, which unfortunately makes them expensive. As a result the people are looking for the one that can provide high quality with an affordable rate which could potentially take over all of their vitals. Therefore i’m bringing something that will meet all your needs, which is Pillshare

What you can expect from Pillshare ?

With Pillshare, you can track all your vitals (blood pressure, body temperature, heart rate, oxygen saturation, and glucose level) that ensures higher accuracy results which will monitor your body throughout the day continuously. In addition to that, it helps you to take your pills at the right time and keeps a record of it. This will sent out alerts, to you and your family listed, which depends on the duration you want to get notified.Still if you miss to take your pills, it will sent you a message with the list of medication that you missed at the end of the day.

Once a week you will get a report based on your vitals and pills tracking record that was done over the week. So you will understand how your health is progressing. If you want to share your realtime data to your family or to your doctor you can generate a shareable link that you can sent to them. As soon as they get access to the link, they will be able to track your data in realtime which helps the doctors in remote diagnosing. More features are embedded into this device which you can see at Pillshare.co.
