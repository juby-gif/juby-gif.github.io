Interconnection of Electrical Equipments using Wireless Power Transmission

This was a mini project that I’ve done during my Engineering at University. We developed an idea that uses Wireless Power Transmission Technology for charging electrical appliances. The problem we were facing during the initial stage was the limited power access for the appliances. For example, to charge our mobile phones we have to have a charger with us everytime and even though we had one, still we could connect only one at a time. So we thought about increasing the power accessibility to multiple devices at a time which is how this project gets the pace.

What is Wireless Power Transmission ?

From the term Wireless Power Transmission it makes more sense that transferring power without wires, which is absolutely true. According to Science Direct, Wireless power transfer (WPT) is the transmission of electrical power without wires and is based on technologies using time-varying electric, magnetic, or electromagnetic fields.

When an Analog current supply is given to an inductive current coil, theoretically it generates an electromagnetic field around the coil which is called Electric Flux. Electric Flux is directly proportional to the electric lines of force. As a result, when the current supply is increased, the electrical lines of force increases that leads to a stronger electromagnetic flux around the coil. When another inductive coil is placed within this electrical field, the second coil will generate a power supply or an electromotive force through inductive coupling or technically called as electromagnetic induction.

How power is transferred ?

The concept of developing the WPT technology is on connecting all household appliances to a single power source at a time which is visualized in the image.

According to the image, there’s a source coil which is acting as the core part of this technology, that creates a stronger electromagnetic field and thereby generating electric flux. The source coil will be connected to a power outlet(100-240V, ~1.5A) that creates a stronger magnetic field within certain radius. All the appliances that are placed within this will have power supply in it through inductive coupling. As a result power can be transferred from one to another wirelessly.
