Who am I?

Full Stack Developer, I develop web-apps using the Agile methodology and I am exact in research that I do, to validate customer needs to ensure I am building a product feature that solves a customer pain. This attention to detail, and to my customer’s needs, creates a user experience that puts the customer first. It is important to me, to build products, that help people, and are focused on solving bigger societal issues. Therefore, I’ve developed BioDB, a WebApp that extracts and saves data using Apple Health kit, presents it in easy to understand graphical formats to assist the elderly in monitoring important health indicators and in monitoring more specific things like how taking new medications impacts their wellbeing.

As a Frontend developer, I was Lead Developer for the BCI Dev Team, that built the first BCI Community Portal to support early stage entrepreneurs to find likeminded people and cofounders that share the same values and principles as they do. We built it to provide the sustained resources and support, that many early founders do not have when they are a pre-revenue bootstrapped startup. During this work, I’ve utilized HTML5, TypeScript, JavaScript (ES5 and ES6), CSS3 and React.js for the frontend work and Python and the Django Rest Framework for the backend while developing both WordPress websites and WebApps. This work involved coordinating 3 other developers and project managing the workflow to ensure it met agreed timelines. The community portal involved setting up 3 different WordPress sites and a significant amount of integrations with other platforms and technologies (Feedly; MailChimp; Mighty Networks; HubSpot etc.) to ensure the kind of high quality UX that all BCI products are known for.

I am a solutions driven tech entrepreneur, who values solving real world problems. Reach out to me if you are interested in collaborating or connecting around some shared ideas about how we can make the world a better place.
